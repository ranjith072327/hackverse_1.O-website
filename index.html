About the Hackathon

Why students should participate in Hackverse 1.0

Hackverse 1.0 is an exciting 24-hour hackathon that provides a platform for students to showcase their programming talent, problem-solving ability, and innovation using modern AI-powered development tools. The event is designed to promote teamwork, practical learning, rapid prototyping, and creative thinking. Participants will work in teams to build solutions that address real-world problems and demonstrate their implementation before the evaluation panel.
